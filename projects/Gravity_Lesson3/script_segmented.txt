What if gravity was a ghostly force, waiting to pull you into its depths? In a dark, eerie orchard at night, a shadowy figure looms beneath an apple tree. A single, ripe apple glistens ominously in the moonlight—would you dare to discover its secret? Suddenly, the apple drops to the ground. This is gravity, the invisible force pulling everything toward each other. But why does the apple fall? According to Sir Isaac Newton, gravity pulls with a force of 9.8 m/s²—every second, it accelerates downwards, as if the Earth itself is reaching out to claim everything!

But what if gravity vanished? Everything would float away, lost to the void—an empty, haunting silence. Try this at home: drop a ball and count the seconds until it hits the ground. Feel that pull? Remember, when you witness an apple fall, you’re experiencing a whisper from the universe. What other secrets lie in the shadows?
