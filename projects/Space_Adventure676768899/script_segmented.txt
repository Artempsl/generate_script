Dr. Kate Morgan stood on the desolate lunar surface, shadows stretching long across the gray dust. "Days lost in this void. Just me and the stars," she whispered, fatigue etched on her face. Suddenly, a rhythmic pulse interrupted the eerie silence. The console beeped, a mysterious signal glowing on the screen. "What is that?" she murmured, leaning closer.

Flashbacks erupted in her mind—laughter with friends, family warmth. "I left everything behind… for this." The signal flickered, chaos erupting as alarms blared. Kate’s heart raced, her eyes darting. "The signal... it feels alive. I’m losing myself."

Recording a log, her voice trembled, and the camera pulled back, revealing her isolation. The pulse intensified, a bright glow enveloping her. "Do I respond? Or risk waking something I can't understand?"
