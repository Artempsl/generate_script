Gunfire echoed through the desolate streets as Jack sprinted, adrenaline surging. Dust and debris danced in the air, his heart racing with each near-miss. Somewhere, in the shadows, Lila hid, her eyes wide with fear as flashes of their last happy moment together flickered in her mind.
