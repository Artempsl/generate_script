Commander Max stood in his sleek space suit, the red dust of Mars swirling ominously around him. "Mars is dangerous," he declared with a furrowed brow, the weight of the mission heavy on his shoulders. Venturing into a dark cave, eerie sounds echoed. Heart racing, Max's face twisted in terror. Suddenly, a giant kitten-like creature leaped from the shadows, landing on him with playful enthusiasm. Max, fumbling with a laser pointer, shouted, “Stay back!” The monster, undeterred, chased the light, mirroring Max's panic in a slapstick dance of chaos.

“ZAP, what is it?” he yelled, as the AI chimed in, “Analyzing absurdity levels.” Just as Max thought he was cornered, the creature offered a Martian toy, nuzzling him for a hug. “Congratulations, Commander,” ZAP deadpanned, “you’ve befriended a Martian.
